About Lamborghini

Driving innovation and excellence for over 50 years

About Us

Lamborghini is a renowned Italian luxury car manufacturer, known for pushing the boundaries of automotive engineering and design. Founded in 1963, Lamborghini continues to deliver high-performance vehicles that epitomize speed, elegance, and innovation. Our commitment to excellence is reflected in every model, ensuring that every car that leaves our factory is a masterpiece.

Our History

Founded by Ferruccio Lamborghini in 1963, Automobili Lamborghini S.p.A. has a rich legacy that spans over half a century. Originally established to compete with Ferrari, Lamborghini quickly earned a reputation for producing bold, avant-garde designs and powerful, high-performance cars.

Throughout the decades, Lamborghini has introduced groundbreaking models like the Miura, Countach, and Aventador, each setting new standards in performance and design. Today, Lamborghini continues to innovate with hybrid technologies and ultra-high-performance supercars.
